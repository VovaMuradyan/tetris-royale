Tetris Royale Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Summary

Tetris Royale is a multiplayer game that communicates with its backend over WebSocket and uses Firebase Analytics. The app does not require an account to play.

Information we collect

  • App interactions and basic analytics events via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device or other IDs used by Firebase for analytics and app diagnostics.
  • Network metadata necessary to operate the game service, such as IP address and connection timestamps.

How we use information

  • To operate multiplayer matches and keep the game server authoritative.
  • To detect errors, abuse, and cheating.
  • To measure app stability and usage trends.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared with service providers only as needed to run the game and analytics infrastructure, including Firebase and the hosting provider.

Retention

Match logs and analytics are retained only as long as needed for operation, debugging, security, and legal compliance.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Google Play listing or the project repository.
